Corporate Wellness at SALVEO

Welcome to Salveo's Corporate Health Transformation program. Salveo offers you a comprehensive health management program, that covers all aspects of health and wellness - helping to reduce the health risk to the organization. This results in higher productivity, better cognitive performance and lower stress levels - which in turn has a direct impact on the organization's revenue and profits. Taking into consideration that health and wellness requirements can vary from organization to organization, Salveo's end to end health solutions are modular in nature, and can be completely aligned to the organization's corporate and HR strategies.
