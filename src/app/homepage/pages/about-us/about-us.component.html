About Us

Welcome to the Virtual Hospital

Started at Bengaluru in September 2017, Salveo, which means “Good Health” in Latin, is India’s first virtual hospital.

Salveo Healthcare Solutions is a next generation ISO 9001 certified digital company. We are a digital healthcare company, which aims to harness the power of technology to deliver quality yet cost-effective services and solutions to you and your family. Managed by a team of highly experienced healthcare professionals, technocrats and business leaders, Salveo’s competitive edge is in providing timely and reliable care. Our flagship product in the form of digital platform “MySalveo” provides the unique ‘virtual hospital’ experience, making quality healthcare accessible to you at the click of a button, and in the comfort of your home. Complementing the same are our physical spaces, Salveo Care Centers, offering personalized treatment solutions. Currently, 12 Centers are operational in Bengaluru, with more underway.

Our Vision

Transforming healthcare from a reactive state to a preventive and proactive state, by using intelligent technology.
